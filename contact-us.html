Business Hours & Response Times

Our support infrastructure operates 24/7 for urgent issues such as account security or transaction errors. For regular queries and feedback, you can expect a comprehensive response within 24 hours. Email responses typically arrive within 2-4 hours, while our live chat and WhatsApp channels provide near-instantaneous assistance.

Stay Updated

Don't forget to follow our official Telegram channel for the latest news, updates, maintenance schedules, and exclusive announcements. Staying connected ensures you never miss out on important platform changes.

Tips for Faster Resolution

To help our support agents resolve your issues as quickly as possible, please include the following when reaching out:

  • Your registered Paglu Game account ID or username.
  • A clear description of the issue or query.
  • Relevant screenshots or screen recordings (especially for transaction or gameplay issues).
  • The time and date the issue occurred.
